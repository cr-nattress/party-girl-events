# Interactive guest experiences (late-night, activations, personalization)

## Purpose of this brief
This README is an internal “blog post brief” for a post about interactive guest experiences—how to pick activations that feel fun (not gimmicky) and work for your venue and crowd.

## Why this topic is trending (signals to reference)
- **Guest experience is a top planning priority**: The Knot references the importance of guests being well taken care of and having a good time.
  - Source: https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-venue-trends
- **Interactive experiences are called out as part of hyper-personalization**: Zola notes a move toward interactive guest experiences (examples include live painting and tarot readers).
  - Source: https://www.zola.com/expert-advice/the-first-look-report-2026
- **Tech-enabled participation is rising**: The Knot highlights QR codes for photo uploads as a way to make weddings more interactive.
  - Source: https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-technology-trends

## Audience + intent
- **Primary reader**
