# Engagement ring trends (lab-grown, vintage, colored stones)

## Purpose of this brief
This README is an internal “blog post brief” for a trend-focused engagement ring article that’s practical for proposers and ring shoppers.

## Why this topic is trending (signals to reference)
- **More couples shop together earlier**: Zola reports 55% of couples shop for rings together, and 68% mutually decide when to get engaged.
  - Source: https://www.zola.com/expert-advice/the-first-look-report-2026
- **Shift toward bolder styles**: Brides reports chunky bands and bold settings replacing dainty designs for 2025.
  - Source: https://www.brides.com/engagement-ring-trends-2025-8761563
- **Specific setting and cut trends**: Brides highlights bezel settings, three-stone rings, and interest shifting away from ovals toward other cuts (emerald/princess/radiant), plus elongated antique-cut diamonds.
  - Source: https://www.brides.com/engagement-ring-trends-2025-8761563
- **Color is a personalization lever**: Brides highlights colorful center stones (emerald green, peach, purple) as a trend.
  - Source: https://www.brides.com/engagement-ring-trends-2025-8761563
