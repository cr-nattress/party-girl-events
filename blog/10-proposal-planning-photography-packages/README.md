- **Pre-proposal planning is increasingly normal**: Zola reports that 68% of couples mutually decide when to get engaged, 55% shop for rings together, and 28% build mood boards before “yes,” with 19% entering full planning mode before being technically engaged.
  - Source: https://www.zola.com/expert-advice/the-first-look-report-2026
- **Social-first documentation expectations are high**: Zola reports 87% of couples make wedding planning choices based on something they saw on social media.
  - Source: https://www.zola.com/expert-advice/the-first-look-report-2026
- **“Courthouse moment” + extra documented moments** are increasingly curated and photographed (a proxy signal that couples also want proposals documented in a polished way).
  - Source: https://www.theknot.com/content/new-wedding-trends
